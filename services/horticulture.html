Horticulture

Home Our Services Horticulture

SDFM services provides Gardener to install and maintain the flowers, trees and green spaces on your property / Offices.
Our responsibilities are monitoring the health of all plants and greenscapes, watering and feeding plants, trimming trees and shrubs, fertilizing and mowing lawns, weeding gardens and keeping green spaces and walkways clear of debris and litter.

We know how to use and maintain landscaping equipment, including mowers, trimmers and fertilizers, while following health and safety regulations.

    OUR RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Install and maintain seasonal plants
  • Mow, trim and fertilize green spaces
  • Mulch, edge and weed gardens
  • Prune and trim trees and bushes
  • Maintain all gardening equipment and machinery, like mowers, trimmers and leaf blowers
  • Monitor and maintain the health of plants
  • Deal with pest problems that could damage plants
  • Keep gardens and green spaces clear of debris and litter

Our Gardners are experienced, Familiarity with landscaping design.

We are also providing rental plants for offices.
