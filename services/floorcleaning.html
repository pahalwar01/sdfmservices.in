Floor Cleaning

Home Our Services Floor Cleaning

Floors are the first thing that gets noticed when your guests/employees or customers walk into your premises. Well maintained and cleaned floors give an exuberant feeling to your presence in that space. Be it your guests/employees, customer or clients, well-kept floors would positively influence their views on your brand. Our advanced floor cleaning services help enhance the ambience of your office/Residental space.



Waxing, Buffing & Polishing of floors

  • Waxing: Floor waxing assists in sealing the porous hardwood surface, shields it from stains, conceals scrapes and indentations, and boosts the floor’s shine. It looks exquisite to make your floor glow, and waxing can precisely smooth out the imperfections. Moreover, waxing aid the ceramic tiles’ floor, as well in making it waterproof, stain- resistant, and forms a non-slippery surface.
  • Buffing: After waxing, buffing the floors with our advanced Floor Buffer Machine would further help give a perfect shining to the flooring, thus providing a professional look to the floors of your offices/Houses. This buffer machine has rotating pads that work at a slow pace to eliminate any smudges or nicks the floor might have.
  • Polishing: Floor polishing or burnishing is similar to buffing, except the machines work at a much higher speed, and the process is more extensive. The non-polished floor looks dull and unclean; therefore, SDFM services can help you give a glowy look to your office surfaces through our advanced machinery.
